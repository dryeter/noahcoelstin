Noah Coelestin

As Joint PhD student in the groups of Prof. Pooi See Lee at NTU and Prof. Patrick Unwin at the University of Warwick, Noah Al-Shamery normally works on the electrochemical and spectroscopic investigations of polymer systems. Having taught as a certified college level tutor in courses like Statistical Thermodynamics and Kinetics in Germany, he was always curious about how students self-reflected on their own work. Thus, he was more than happy to work with Prof. Ibrahim Yeter and Dr. Eileen Fong on analyzing student self-reflections throughout an engineering NTU course from 2022-2023.
